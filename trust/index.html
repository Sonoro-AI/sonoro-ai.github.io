b.System architecture

A layered orchestration stack. Six tiers. One audit trail. Built for governance, AI safety and scale.

Sonoro is architected as six discrete layers — User, Interface, Intelligence, Knowledge, Data, Integration — bound by a continuous Governance plane. Every recommendation is traceable to its source. Every data exchange is permissioned. Every AI output is bounded by an approved knowledge graph. This is not optional in care. It is the product.
