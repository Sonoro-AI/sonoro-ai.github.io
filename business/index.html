Part VI The Business

Sequencing, revenue, and the data flywheel that makes this defensible.

a.MVP roadmap

Four phases. Twelve months from signal to scale.

The roadmap is sequenced by commercial defensibility, not technical convenience. Each phase ships a usable product surface and creates the data asset that powers the next phase. By the time competitors notice, the moat is already built.

01.Phase one

Ask Marco foundation

Establish the conversational surface, the approved content library, and the first signal: every question asked, every action taken, every module recommended.

b.Commercial model

Eight revenue surfaces. One platform. Compound defensibility.

Sonoro doesn't pick a business model. It builds infrastructure that several business models can run on simultaneously. SaaS subscription anchors the relationship. Content, training, recruitment and analytics expand it. The longer a customer stays, the more surfaces they activate.

i.Care home subscription

Per-home monthly licence covering Ask Marco, Menu Intelligence and Happiness Algorithm. The commercial anchor.

Anchor SaaS
ii.Group licence

Multi-home enterprise agreement with cross-portfolio benchmarking, regional dashboards and operator-level governance.

Enterprise expansion
iii.MPW Academy upsell

Premium content access, certification pathways, module bundles. Every Ask Marco answer is a soft sell.

Content margin
iv.Learn Care Excel training

Routed care training sales — compliance, mandatory, dignity-in-mealtimes. Embedded inside the platform's daily flow.

Training revenue
v.TLC apprenticeship

Funded apprenticeship origination — eligibility, application, employer engagement, levy guidance.

Funded learning
vi.Curam recruitment

Per-placement revenue from operators recruiting through Sonoro's pre-qualified candidate flow.

Placement margin
vii.ATS-powered assessment

Per-candidate Marco Standard scoring sold to operators outside the core subscription.

Per-seat data
viii.Analytics & benchmarking

Aggregated, anonymised industry benchmarks sold to investors, regulators and supplier partners.

Commercial reality

Sonoro is not a discretionary spend. It is a margin instrument.

  • — Reduce food waste by 10–20%

If Sonoro improves food quality and reduces waste, it pays for itself multiple times over.

c.The data flywheel

Sonoro improves with every home, every meal, every hire.

Most platforms degrade as they scale — more edge cases, more support overhead, more dilution. Sonoro does the opposite. Every home that joins, every menu that's scored, every candidate matched feeds the next decision. The product gets sharper. The data gets richer. The moat gets deeper.

i.More homesEach operator joining brings a new menu, a new resident profile set, a new workforce signal.
ii.More dataMenu performance, plate waste, family feedback, candidate outcomes, training completion.
iii.Better scoringThe Happiness Algorithm and Marco Standard sharpen with every new signal absorbed.
iv.Better outcomesSharper recommendations. Higher resident satisfaction. Fewer recruitment misfires.
v.More adoptionBetter outcomes drive referral, retention and the next operator signing on.
Defensibility

This is how Sonoro becomes defensible. Not through a feature. Through a flywheel that nobody outside the network can replicate.
